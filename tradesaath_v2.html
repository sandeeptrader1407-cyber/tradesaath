FAQ

What file formats are supported?▼
We support CSV, Excel (.xlsx, .xls), PDF trade reports, and screenshots (PNG, JPG). Compatible with Zerodha, Angel One, Upstox, any broker export format.
What markets and currencies?▼
All major markets: NSE, BSE, NYSE, NASDAQ, LSE, ASX, SGX, Forex, Crypto, Commodities. Currency is auto-detected from your file and shown accordingly.
Is my data private?▼
100% private. Your trade data is processed locally and never shared with anyone. Cross-user insights are fully anonymized and aggregated.
What is the Vicious Cycle?▼
The Vicious Cycle is our proprietary 10-stage framework that maps every trade to an emotional stage: from Good Trade → Overconfidence → Larger Position → Market Against → Hope → Averaging Down → Panic Exit → Revenge Trade → Decision Fatigue → FOMO Re-entry.
Can I cancel?▼
Yes, cancel anytime from your account settings. Single reports are one-time — no subscription. Pro plans can be cancelled before the next billing cycle.
